Strength in numbers.

Centralize the most important elements of your night out







Start Together

Before you head out, get on board your raft. Using Bluetooth, you can instantly gain access to all of your friend's contact information. Set safe zones. Make a game plan.


Enjoy Your Night

Spend your night as you wish! A quick glance at your phone shows you not only where all of your crewmates are, but also what their status is.


Time To Go

When you're ready to go, call an Uber for yourself or join your friends on their ride home. Don't worry about forgetting to tell anyone though, when a driver is called we'll automatically notify everyone in your raft for you.


End Wherever

When your night has come to a close, a few quick taps on your screen will let your friends know that it ended safely. If it happens that you need their help, everyone will be notified immediately.

Previous Next

Built-in Google Maps allows you to know exactly where your friends are at all times. Setting up a pre-defined radius and chosing your own safe zones ensures that no member of your crew will ever fall overboard.


Ready to head home? Contact Uber from within liferaft. Your friends will not only be instantly notified that you're heading out, but they'll also be invited to join you on the journey home.


Our implementation of Firebase ensures the fastest and most reliable communicaiton using could services. Thanks to Firebase, you're able to chat with everyone in your raft at once, without having to revert to other communcaiton platforms.
